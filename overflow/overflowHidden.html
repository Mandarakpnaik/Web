> Punith Rajkumar
Puneeth Rajkumar (1975-2021) was an Indian actor, singer, film producer, and television presenter, primarily known for his work in Kannada cinema. Fondly called "Appu" and the "Power Star" by his fans, he was one of the most popular and highest-paid actors in the industry. He died on October 29, 2021, at the age of 46, due to a massive heart attack.
